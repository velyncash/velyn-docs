# FAQ

This document answers common questions about Velyn.Cash.

---

## What is Velyn.Cash?

Velyn.Cash is a modern neobank & payments platform focused on clear UX, security-by-design, and practical everyday payments.

---

## Is Velyn.Cash live?

Velyn.Cash is under **active development**.  
Some features are implemented at UI/testing stages, and integrations are being built incrementally.

---

## Why is development public?

Public development improves transparency:
- visible progress
- public documentation as a source of truth
- accountability through commits and CI checks

---

## What payment methods will Velyn support?

Planned support includes:
- QR payments
- Contactless (NFC)
- Card-based transactions (virtual / physical planned)
- RFID flows for controlled scenarios (planned)

Availability can vary by phase and region.

---

## Do I need a token to use Velyn.Cash?

Token-related mechanics will be documented separately.  
Core product experience is designed to remain accessible and user-first.

---

## Is user data shared with third parties?

By default, no.  
Privacy and data minimization are core principles. Any integrations will be clearly documented.

---

## How can I join beta access?

Private beta is invitation-based during early development.  
Public beta will be announced as the platform stabilizes.

---

## Where can I follow updates?

- GitHub organization: https://github.com/velyncash
