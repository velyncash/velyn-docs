# Velyn.Cash Roadmap

This roadmap outlines the development phases of Velyn.Cash.
Execution is incremental, feedback-driven, and focused on long-term delivery.

---

## Phase 1 — Foundation (Completed)

Focused on building a strong strategic and operational base.

- Business strategy research and market positioning
- Core team formation and role definition
- Social media setup and brand identity
- Official landing page release

---

## Phase 2 — Platform Development (Ongoing)

Transition from foundation into real product execution.

- Velyn.Cash platform development
- Tokenomics design and public release
- Internal private testing (team only)
- Private beta testing by invitation
- Launch of Velyn.Cash native token on Solana

---

## Phase 3 — Public Launch & Expansion (Planned)

Driving adoption, feedback, and ecosystem growth.

- Public beta release
- User feedback and product iteration
- Multi-platform mobile release (Android and iOS)
- Ecosystem expansion rollout plan

---

## Execution Principles

- Build step by step
- Ship incrementally and iterate continuously
- Prioritize clarity, security, and real-world usability
- Focus on long-term execution over short-term hype
