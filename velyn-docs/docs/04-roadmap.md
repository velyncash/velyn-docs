# Velyn.Cash Roadmap

This roadmap outlines the planned development phases of **Velyn.Cash**.
Execution is incremental and built around real progress, testing, and iteration.

The roadmap may evolve as development and feedback continue.

---

## Phase 1 — Foundation (Completed)

This phase focused on building a strong strategic and operational base.

- Business strategy research and market positioning
- Core team formation and role definition
- Social media setup and brand identity
- Official landing page release

**Outcome:** Velyn.Cash established a clear direction, brand identity, and execution readiness.

---

## Phase 2 — Platform Development (Ongoing)

This phase transitions from foundation into real product execution.

- Velyn.Cash platform development (web + mobile)
- Tokenomics design and public release
- Internal private testing (team only)
- Private beta testing (invitation-based)
- Launch of the Velyn.Cash native token on Solana

**Focus:** shipping core functionality, validating real flows, and hardening security and reliability.

---

## Phase 3 — Public Launch & Expansion (Planned)

This phase drives adoption, feedback loops, and ecosystem growth.

- Public beta release
- User feedback and rapid iteration
- Multi-platform mobile release (Android and iOS)
- Ecosystem expansion rollout plan

**Focus:** stable public availability, continuous improvement, and scalable growth.

---

## Execution Principles

- Build step by step
- Ship incrementally and improve continuously
- Prioritize clarity, security, and real-world usability
- Focus on long-term execution over short-term hype
